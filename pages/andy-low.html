MYKI INSPECTOR ALERT APP

Frustrated with the myki inspector facebook page not updating fast enough for any uni student to bypass a fine, Andy created the Myki Inspector Alert App (available on iOS and Android). Tram number, route, time and location can be logged to a statewide database for real-time updates for the everyday inspector dodger. Using data from the Public Transport Victoria’s tram schedules, the app marks every operating tram on a map, colour coding trams as red once an inspector has been reported on it within the past 30 minutes. The app uses the user’s location data to give notifications for when they are approaching a myki-inspector-infested tram. Andy is now working on prototypes for small location trackers to directly attach to myki inspectors!
