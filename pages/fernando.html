About Fernando
Fernando is an adventurer from Transylvania. He may have had a few close run-ins with vampires, but despite his near death experiences, he is an avid lover of Twilight. Now, Fernando is taking a career change, using his adventurer tenacity to transverse the coding world. Fernando will achieve anything he puts his mind to, regardless of the time it may take.

‘Twilight.com’ - the largest Twilight Fanbase site

After one of his Friday night Twilight Saga marathons, Fernando decided to capitalise on his obsession and created a fully functioning website for Twilight fans from all over the world. Complete with an active forum for all things Twilight related, not-so-official merchandise store, and weekly fanart competitions, Twilight.com became one of the most visited fanbase sites in the world.

Recovery of Henry VIII’s Lost Crown

Did you see the news of a treasure hunter finding a long lost crown? That was Fernando on one of his journeys! This crown is a symbol of potent power and religious authority, and the fame it brought him inspired him for his next project…

VentureMap

After garnering a huge fanbase from his crown discovery, Fernando wanted to make his passions of adventuring more accessible to his admirers. So he created VentureMap! VentureMap is a phone app which researches local myths and urban legends based on the user location, creating estimates for the location of local artefacts. Now, the average walk-taker can transform into a treasure hunting hero!

BigFoot Tracker

Fernando has seen lots of feet throughout his lifetime, but not any big feet… Or should he say, Bigfoots. His lifelong dream of hunting one down prompted him to create BigFoot Tracker, a database that allows users to upload any mythical beast sightings. Any pursuer of Bigfoot, or the Loch Ness monster, or mermaids, or any other rare creatures can log their sighting on the website, allowing the next sighting to be predicted.
