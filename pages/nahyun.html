Kanvas

Sick of the constant pile of lectures from her first year subjects (that she should have watched a month before), Nahyun developed a Learning Management System of her own that would help her feel less overwhelmed from the consequences of her own procrastination. Almost an exact replica of Canvas LMS, Kanvas allows watching lectures beyond the standard 2x speed - up to 10x speed! (in convenient 0.25x increments) This condenses an entire semester’s worth of lectures into just 3.6 hours each subject. Kanvas also comes with a speech-to-text function, allowing Nahyun to skip lectures completely and simply just read a transcript. Note writing has never troubled Nahyun since the conception of her project, due to the Note-Copy feature which converts the lecturer’s handwriting into conveniently formatted word documents. Not much actual studying has been done, but at least Nahyun feels productive!

PenGoogle

Nahyun and Ashlyn are dedicated lovers of penguins, starting a penguin trend in their projects. Nahyun developed ‘Pengoogle’, a search engine for penguin-related information for penguin loves. Pengoogle, was an idea that formed in her head when her Google searches failed to give her a sufficient amount of penguin related data during one of her trips to Antarctica. Complete with ‘Pengoogle Translate’, with the capability of English-Penguinese (Simplified and Traditional) translation, Pengoogle is one of the most powerful Antarctica-based search engines available today.
